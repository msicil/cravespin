Restaurant roulette for iPhone

Find a meal in one spin

Can’t decide where to eat? CraveSpin loads nearby restaurants, spins a gold-trimmed wheel, and picks a winner. Open directions in Maps or reserve when the restaurant supports it.

Available on the App Store for iPhone.

Spin the wheel

Pull the lever or tap Spin. CraveSpin animates a slot-style reel and lands on one restaurant from your list.

Filter your options

Set distance, minimum rating, price level, and open-now. Search near your current location or another city.

Go from winner to table

See photos, ratings, and hours on the winner card. Open Google Maps for directions or visit the restaurant website to reserve.

Home-screen widget

Add the Spin widget for one-tap access. Tap to open CraveSpin and spin again when you’re ready.

Need help?

Questions, feedback, or something not working? We’re happy to help.
